What is Ironclad Clickwrap?

Ironclad Clickwrap is a simple yet powerful solution for embedding enforceable agreements in your website or app. It ensures users explicitly agree to terms and conditions before proceeding with transactions.

Why Use Clickwrap?

  • **Legal Compliance**: Ensure users explicitly agree to your terms.
  • **User Experience**: Seamlessly integrates into your website or app.
  • **Customization**: Easily match your brand's style and needs.
